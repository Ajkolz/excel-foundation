Education Program

Every child deserves a quality education

Our Education Program is the cornerstone of The Excel Foundation's work. We believe that access to quality education is both a right and a foundation for everything else — economic stability, health, confidence, and community leadership.

Through scholarships, school infrastructure projects, teacher training, and learning materials distribution, we are breaking down the barriers that keep children out of classrooms.

Our flagship sub-project — Back to School — tackles one of the most immediate barriers: the cost of school supplies, uniforms, and registration fees at the start of each academic year.

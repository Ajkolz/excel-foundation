Why volunteer?
More than just helping out

Make a tangible difference

Your time and skills directly touch the lives of children, families, and communities who need it most. You will see the impact firsthand.

Join a faith community

Our volunteer team is a family — united by faith, driven by compassion. You will build meaningful relationships that last beyond any single event.

Grow your skills

From project management to community outreach, teaching to healthcare support — volunteering with us is an opportunity to develop and apply real skills.

Ready to serve?
Let's get started.

Fill in the form and our volunteer coordinator will reach out within 3 working days to discuss how you can plug into our upcoming programs and events.

Flexible commitment
We work around your schedule. As little as one event per quarter qualifies you as an active volunteer.
Onboarding and orientation
All new volunteers receive an orientation session before their first deployment so you are always confident and prepared.
Volunteer certificate
Active volunteers receive an official Excel Foundation certificate of service — recognised for CVs, NYSC records, and professional portfolios.
All backgrounds welcome
You do not need a specific qualification to volunteer — just a willing heart, reliable character, and the desire to serve.

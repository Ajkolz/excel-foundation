Will I get a receipt for my donation?
Yes. Email us after transferring with your name and amount, and we will send you a donation receipt and a quarterly impact update showing exactly how your gift was used.
Is my donation tax-deductible?
The Excel Foundation is a registered NGO under the Corporate Affairs Commission. Please contact us for a formal donation letter to support your tax records.
Can I donate in foreign currency?
Yes — contact us at [update: email] and we will provide international transfer details (USD, GBP, EUR accounts available on request).
Can I donate to a specific program?
Absolutely. Include the program name (e.g. "Back to School 2026") in your transfer narration, or email us to earmark your gift for a specific project.
